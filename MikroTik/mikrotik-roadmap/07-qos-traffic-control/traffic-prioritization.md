## What this covers

Traffic prioritization assigns importance.<br>
Not all traffic is equal.

## Key concepts

- Latency-sensitive traffic
- Priority levels
- Queue interaction

## Why it matters in MikroTik

Voice and gaming need low latency.<br>
Bulk traffic can wait.

## Common mistakes

- Prioritizing everything
- No clear traffic classification

## Real-world example

VoIP choppy at peak hours.<br>
Bulk traffic not deprioritized.
