## What this covers

Advanced VLAN uses filtering and rules.<br>
Bridge VLAN filtering is mandatory.

## Key concepts

- VLAN table
- PVID
- Ingress filtering

## Why it matters in MikroTik

Correct VLAN filtering offloads work to hardware.<br>
Performance depends on it.

## Common mistakes

- Enabling VLAN filtering without planning
- Forgetting management VLAN
