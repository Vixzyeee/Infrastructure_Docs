## What this covers

Neighbor discovery finds devices on LAN.<br>
Useful for management, risky for security.

## Key concepts

- MNDP and LLDP
- Interface scope
- Discovery visibility

## Why it matters in MikroTik

Unrestricted discovery exposes network info.<br>
Should be limited.

## Common mistakes

- Leaving discovery enabled on WAN
- Assuming it is harmless

## Real-world example

Router visible from ISP side.<br>
Discovery not restricted.
