## What this covers

Logging records system events.<br>
It is the first debugging tool.

## Key concepts

- Log topics
- Log levels
- Local vs remote logging

## Why it matters in MikroTik

Most failures leave traces.<br>
Without logs, context is lost.

## Common mistakes

- Logging nothing
- Logging everything

## Real-world example

Router rebooted unexpectedly.<br>
Logs reveal power issue.
