## What this covers

Graphing visualizes resource usage over time.<br>
Trends matter more than snapshots.

## Key concepts

- Interface graphs
- CPU and memory graphs
- Historical analysis

## Why it matters

Trends predict failures.<br>
Sudden spikes explain incidents.

## Common mistakes

- Not enabling graphing
- Short retention periods

## Real-world example

Router slows weekly.<br>
Graph shows scheduled job spike.
