## What this covers

Switch chip handles hardware switching.<br>
Not all MikroTik devices have one.

## Key concepts

- Switch groups
- Port mapping
- Feature limitations

## Why it matters in MikroTik

Switch chip defines performance ceiling.<br>
Design must match hardware.

## Common mistakes

- Treating router like enterprise switch
- Ignoring chip documentation

## Real-world example

VLAN works but slow.<br>
Traffic not handled by switch chip.
