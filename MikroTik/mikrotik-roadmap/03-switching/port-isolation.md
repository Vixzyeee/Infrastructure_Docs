## What this covers

Port isolation prevents ports from talking directly.<br>
Traffic must go through router or uplink.

## Key concepts

- Isolated ports
- Community ports
- Protected ports

## Why it matters in MikroTik

Prevents lateral movement on LAN.<br>
Improves basic security.

## Common mistakes

- Assuming VLAN replaces isolation
- Blocking required services accidentally

## Real-world example

Clients cannot ping each other.<br>
Isolation works as designed.
