## What this covers

Routing filters control route exchange.<br>
They protect routing logic.

## Key concepts

- Accept vs discard
- Prefix matching
- Attribute modification

## Why it matters in MikroTik

Dynamic routing without filters is reckless.<br>
Filters are mandatory.

## Common mistakes

- Accepting all routes
- No outbound filtering

## Real-world example

Routing table explodes.<br>
ISP sends full routes.
