## What this covers

VRRP provides gateway redundancy.<br>
Virtual IP floats between routers.

## Key concepts

- Master and backup
- Priority
- Preemption

## Why it matters

Clients see one gateway.<br>
Router failure is hidden.

## Common mistakes

- No upstream redundancy
- Split-brain scenarios

## Real-world example

Both routers act as master.<br>
VRRP misconfigured.
