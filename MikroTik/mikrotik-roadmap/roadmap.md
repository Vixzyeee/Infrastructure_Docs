# MikroTik Learning Roadmap

This roadmap is designed for long-term learning and real-world usage of MikroTik RouterOS.

It is structured from fundamentals to advanced operation, automation, and troubleshooting.

The goal is not certification-only knowledge, but practical engineering understanding.

---

## Phase 00 – Fundamentals
Focus:
- Networking fundamentals
- Linux networking concepts
- RouterOS architecture and packet flow

Outcome:
- Understand how traffic works before touching configuration

---

## Phase 01 – Initial Setup
Focus:
- Router initialization
- Security baseline
- WAN connectivity using DHCP Client

Outcome:
- Safely bring a new router online

---

## Phase 02 – Interface Management
Focus:
- Physical and virtual interfaces
- VLAN and bonding concepts
- Interface grouping and roles

Outcome:
- Build clean and scalable interface layout

---

## Phase 03 – Switching
Focus:
- Bridge VLAN filtering
- Hardware offload
- Layer 2 protection

Outcome:
- Use MikroTik as a proper switch without killing CPU

---

## Phase 04 – IP Services
Focus:
- IP addressing
- DHCP Client / Server / Relay
- DNS and NTP services

Outcome:
- Manage IP lifecycle inside the router

---

## Phase 05 – Routing
Focus:
- Static and dynamic routing
- Policy routing
- Failover design

Outcome:
- Control traffic paths reliably

---

## Phase 06 – Firewall and NAT
Focus:
- Firewall architecture
- Connection tracking
- NAT and traffic filtering

Outcome:
- Secure traffic flow without breaking performance

---

## Phase 07 – QoS and Traffic Control
Focus:
- Queue types
- Traffic classification
- Bandwidth prioritization

Outcome:
- Fair and predictable bandwidth usage

---

## Phase 08 – Wireless
Focus:
- Wireless fundamentals
- CAPsMAN
- Point-to-point and multipoint links

Outcome:
- Stable and secure wireless deployment

---

## Phase 09 – Tunneling and VPN
Focus:
- Tunneling protocols
- Secure remote and site-to-site connectivity

Outcome:
- Connect networks across different locations

---

## Phase 10 – Security
Focus:
- Router hardening
- IPsec and certificates
- Attack mitigation

Outcome:
- Reduce attack surface and risk

---

## Phase 11 – High Availability
Focus:
- Gateway failover
- VRRP
- Script-based redundancy

Outcome:
- Network survives component failure

---

## Phase 12 – Monitoring and Logging
Focus:
- Logging and metrics
- SNMP and traffic monitoring

Outcome:
- Detect and analyze issues early

---

## Phase 13 – Scripting and Automation
Focus:
- RouterOS scripting
- API and automation tools

Outcome:
- Reduce manual configuration work

---

## Phase 14 – Performance Optimization
Focus:
- FastTrack and FastPath
- Hardware offloading
- Throughput testing

Outcome:
- Maximize router performance

---

## Phase 15 – Cloud and Virtualization
Focus:
- CHR deployment
- Hybrid networking

Outcome:
- Run MikroTik in modern infrastructure

---

## Phase 16 – Real World Design
Focus:
- ISP, enterprise, and home lab design

Outcome:
- Apply knowledge to real scenarios

---

## Phase 17 – Troubleshooting
Focus:
- Systematic troubleshooting
- Real incident handling

Outcome:
- Fix problems under pressure
