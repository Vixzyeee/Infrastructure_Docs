## What this covers

Recovering from catastrophic failures.<br>
When normal troubleshooting is impossible.

## Key concepts

- Backup restoration
- Config rollback
- Minimal recovery access

## Why it matters

Disasters happen eventually.<br>
Preparedness reduces downtime.

## Common mistakes

- No tested backup
- Relying on memory

## Real-world example

Router factory reset accidentally.<br>
No usable backup exists.
